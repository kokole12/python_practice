The "0-addition" module
=======================

using "add" and "mul"
---------------------

Checks the sum and product from the add and mul function.
First import "add" and "mul" from "0-addition" module

    >>> from 0-addition import add, mul

Now use it:

    >>> add(4, 6)
    10
    >>> add(2, 4)
    6
    >>> mul(3, 4)
    12
    >>> mul('a', 3)
    'aaa'
